Why teams use Safe Ingestion Engine

Traditional scrapers fetch first and leave compliance to your application. Safe Ingestion Engine enforces governance before data reaches your system.

🛡

SSRF Protection

Blocks private, loopback, link-local, multicast, and reserved destinations before outbound fetches.

🧼

PII Redaction

Scrubs common sensitive data before persistence or delivery, helping reduce compliance risk.

📜

robots.txt Enforcement

Applies structural checks before fetches, rather than treating compliance as an afterthought.

⚙️

YAML Policy Engine

Supports per-domain rules, path controls, budgets, and domain-level governance behavior.

🔁

Async Processing

FastAPI + queue + worker architecture supports non-blocking ingestion and cleaner scaling.
